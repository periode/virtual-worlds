  2. World Decadence & Resurrection Benoit Belsot
    Due to climate change, water rising, wars and pandemics that followed, the world almost reach brink of extinction. However, in parallel to all those problem, computation power and great developpers manage to create more and more advance AI until a genius created the perfect one, the one who takes care of everything of what was remaining of the humanity and allows it to live in peace. My project is the museum that show what happened during those previous centuries. This museum is visited in the cyberspace that is directly available through chip connected to the brain of the humans of 2500.

  8. The Growth Initiative Adam Colestock

  9. The Pinnacle Hayden Carey
    The Pinnacle Exhibit celebrates humans from the constraints imposed by work and embraces the benign rule of the machines. This space is a dedication to how far we have come. The rise of machines has left a society where abundance and productivity have reached a pinnacle. No more drudgery. Though humans are experiencing a renaissance of leisure and fun, a spiritual void persists. Exhibits guests become what this world has forgotten.
  10. Temples To Infinity Christopher Wray

  11. From Exploration to Emigration George Faya
    For this project, I tried to envision a less dystopic future where technology succeeds in its promise to improve the human condition by enabling humanity to explore and eventually colonize worlds currently out of reach. Growth, expansion, and – most importantly – our survival is contingent upon our ability to live off-Earth. Even without distant colonization, the stasis we could provide our planet by reducing our burden on it and living in orbit will be crucial to providing an equitable and hopeful future for all humankind.
  12. The Upper Wells Christina Lan
    LoreThis project reimagines a future where today's declining water systems, due to haphazard policies and capitalist economies, are reconstructed entirely. Expanding upon more promising technologies and solutions currently being explored, The Upper Wells hopes to increase visitor awareness and desire to become more involved in the governance and conservation of today's water systems.
  13. Underwater Enrique García
